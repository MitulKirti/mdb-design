S.M.A.R.T


S.M.A.R.T. stands for Syllabus Management, Analysis and Reporting Tool.

As the name suggests S.M.A.R.T. would cater to the need of planning and reporting the day to day academic activities and progress in the syllabus of an educational institution so that adequate and corrective actions can be taken at the appropriate time.
